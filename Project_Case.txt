Project Title
Marketing Funnel, Seller Performance, and Revenue Impact: End-to-End Analytics Project


Client
The client is an online marketplace company that connects sellers with customers.
The company invests in marketing campaigns to acquire potential sellers. These potential sellers enter the funnel as marketing-qualified leads, and some of them become closed deals.
The client wants to understand which marketing channels, landing pages, and lead profiles bring sellers who actually create business value after joining the marketplace.


Main Business Question
Which marketing sources and seller profiles generate the highest-value sellers for the marketplace?


Questions to Answer
1. Which marketing channels generate the best business value?
Analyze lead volume, closed deals, conversion rate, and seller revenue by marketing origin.

2. Which landing pages attract the highest-quality leads?
Analyze landing pages by lead volume, conversion rate, and revenue generated by converted sellers.

3. Which lead profiles should the sales team prioritize?
Analyze lead type, business segment, behaviour profile, and business type to identify the strongest seller profiles.

4. How efficient is the sales process?
Analyze the time between first contact and closed deal, including average conversion time by channel and seller segment.

5. Do converted sellers become active marketplace sellers?
Analyze how many converted sellers actually generate orders and revenue after becoming sellers.

6. Which converted seller groups create the strongest marketplace outcomes?
Analyze revenue, order volume, product categories, review scores, and delivery performance for converted sellers.


From SQL
-- Question 1
-- Which marketing channels generate the best business value across lead volume, conversion rate, active sellers, and revenue?

-- Question 2
-- Which landing pages attract the highest-quality leads based on conversion rate and downstream seller revenue?

-- Question 3
-- Which lead profiles should the sales team prioritize based on conversion probability and expected business impact?

-- Question 4
-- How efficient is the sales process, and which lead segments take the longest to close?

-- Question 5
-- Do converted sellers become active marketplace sellers after closing?

-- Question 6
-- Which converted seller groups create the strongest marketplace outcomes in revenue, orders, product categories, review scores, and delivery performance?
